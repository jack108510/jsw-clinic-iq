# Clinic IQ - Price Management Dashboard

A comprehensive pricing optimization tool for healthcare clinics that analyzes service pricing, identifies inflation lag, and calculates revenue optimization opportunities.

## Features

- **Service List with Current Prices** - Complete view of all clinic services with pricing
- **Inflation Lag Detection** - Automatically flags prices not updated in 2+ years
- **Smart Price Suggestions** - Calculates recommended price increases based on inflation rate
- **Revenue Impact Analysis** - Shows projected revenue increase by category and overall
- **Smart Exclusions** - Automatically excludes wellness exams, vaccinations, and price-sensitive services from increases
- **Approval Workflow** - Track and approve price changes with full audit trail
- **Configurable Settings** - Adjust inflation rate, threshold, and exclusion rules

## Usage

1. **Review Services** - Filter by category or status to find pricing issues
2. **Analyze Impact** - Switch to the Analysis tab to see revenue projections
3. **Approve Changes** - Click "Approve" on services to add to approval queue
4. **Exclude Services** - Mark services as excluded from price increases
5. **Apply Changes** - Export or apply approved price changes

## Settings

- **Annual Inflation Rate** - Default 3.2% (adjustable)
- **Price Update Threshold** - Flag prices older than 2 years (adjustable)
- **Smart Exclusions** - Toggle wellness exams, price-sensitive services
- **Maximum Increase** - Cap individual price increases (default 15%)
